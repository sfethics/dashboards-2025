About candidate committee

State and local laws require candidates to publicly report the financial activity of their campaign committees, including the names of individuals who contribute more than set thresholds in a calendar year and how funds are spent. Candidates in San Francisco's Public Financing Program may receive matching City funds for contributions from local residents. Third-party committees may raise and spend money to support or oppose a candidate, so long as their activity is not coordinated with the candidate's campaign.
