About the Campaign Disclosures Website

This site provides easy access to campaign finance information for San Francisco elections. It is designed to help voters explore how candidates, committees, and third parties raise and spend money in local races, as well as how these activities may influence ballot measures and candidate contests. Visitors can browse summaries of filings, view activity over time, and search contributors across participating committees.

The goal is to make complex campaign finance data easier to understand, encourage public participation, and promote transparency in San Francisco elections.

Who Built This Site

This project is developed and maintained by the San Francisco Ethics Commission, the city department responsible for administering and enforcing local laws related to campaign finance, ethics, conflicts of interest, lobbying, campaign consulting, and major developers.
